About the system

According to the United Nations, amongst all age groups, population in the above 65 years age group is growing at the fastest rate. As much as 90 percent of this elderly population wants to live in their own houses/ communities as long as possible.

However, with deteriorating physical and mental abilities, elderly people are not able to live at home on their own, while nursing homes are expensive. In the current aging society, it is important to help elderly people live at home independently for as long as possible at a low cost.

A non-intrusive solution is chosen, which requires no input from the client, since not all elderly people are comfortable with technology or providing reliable input.

The system does give reminding and warning signals to the client, in order to help them stay healthy, as well as detecting sudden health issues for which caretakers are alerted.

Functionalities

Heart rate monitoring

Fall detection

Smoke and gasleak detection

Medicince checks and reminders
